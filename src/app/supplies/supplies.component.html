Supplies for French Class

The following materials are needed for French class for Grades 7 and 8

  • a binder with 5 dividers (labels on dividers: Référence, A l'écrit, A l'oral, les examens, les bons points)
  • an agenda
  • a French/English dictionary
  • writing tools (pen, pencil, ruler, eraser, markers/pencil crayons

If you are unable to purchase these materials, please let me know.
