What you can do to help your child in French!

  1. Make vocabulary flash cards and play some games with your child.
  2. Encourage your child to review lessons daily.
  3. Ask your child to watch one French television program or read a French book each week
  4. The public library offers books in French. Sign a few out and have your child follow along with the book and listening to how words are pronounced.
  5. Talk about the benefits of learning a language and encourage your child to try to do their best.
