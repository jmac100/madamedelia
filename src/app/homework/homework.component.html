There are lots of things that students can do when they find that they have "no homework"

Here are a few suggestions:

  • review notes of recent lessons
  • review unit vocabulary: make yourself a quiz, pair up with a friend, make flashcards, create a word search and exchange with a friend
  • read a book (visit our school library as we are building our French section in the library) We have some comic books and easy graphic novels - the public library offers many French books
  • listen to French radio (90.3 FM for example)
  • organize loose papers (remember that everything has its place)
